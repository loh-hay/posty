What is Posty?

Posty is a fun, interactive postcard experience. Scan a printed Posty via QR code to reveal a random cat meme, bringing a little joy into your day or someone else's.

How It Works

  1. Scan the Posty with your device.
  2. See a random cat meme pop up on your screen.
  3. Save your favorite surprise or enjoy the next one!

Fun Extras

Each Posty is designed with soft colors, heart animations, and playful buttons to make the experience lighthearted and interactive.
